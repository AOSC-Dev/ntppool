NTP Pool mailing lists

The mailing lists

Currently, there are three mailing lists for the pool.ntp.org project: the discussion list timekeepers@fortytwo.ch (subscribe, archive), the announcement list timekeepers-announce@fortytwo.ch (subscribe, archive) and the development list timekeepers-bulletin@fortytwo.ch (subscribe, archive). The discussion list is, of course, mainly for those interested in the project, while the announcements list is intended for server operators (or users of the pool.ntp.org service) who do not wish to be more deeply involved with the development and maintenance of the project. There is no need to subscribe to both lists - announcements will be sent to both lists (if I don't forget it, that is). Of course, general discussions about ntp should take place on the comp.protocols.time.ntp Usenet newsgroup.
