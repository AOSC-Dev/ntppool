pool.ntp.org server statistics

As announced in this message and further explained here, the time servers in the pool.ntp.org project are monitored, and a scoring system is used to determine the servers which are eliglible for the DNS rotation. Of the good servers, a random subset of 15 servers is chosen for the pool.ntp.org DNS name and changed every hour so that the DNS answers stay small enough to fit in UDP packets, and to achieve a good load balancing even when some caching nameservers do not rotate their entries in pool.ntp.org.
